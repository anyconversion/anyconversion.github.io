Acres to Square Feet Converter

Definition

An acre (ac) is a unit of land area used in the imperial and US customary systems. One acre equals 43,560 square feet. The square foot (ft²) is defined as the area of a square with sides of 1 foot, commonly used in the United States and other countries that use the imperial system.

History

The acre has medieval origins, historically defined as the area of land that could be plowed in one day by a yoke of oxen. It was standardized in the British Weights and Measures Act of 1824. The square foot dates back to ancient civilizations and was formalized in the imperial system, continuing in use in the United States after its independence.
