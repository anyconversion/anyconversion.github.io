Purpose

The Area Conversions category is designed for those working with land, construction, or spatial analysis—whether for real estate, agriculture, or design—offering tools to convert between metric, imperial, and traditional units seamlessly.

History

Area units have roots in practical measurement: square meters and kilometers from the metric system, square feet and yards from English custom, acres and roods from medieval farming, hectares from 19th-century metric adoption, chains and furlongs from surveying, and square inches from precision tasks. This category unites these legacies.
