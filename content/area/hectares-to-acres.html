Hectares to Acres Converter

Definition

Hectare (ha) is a metric unit of area equal to 10,000 square meters or 100 ares, commonly used for measuring land. Acre (ac) is an imperial and US customary unit of land area equal to 43,560 square feet. One hectare equals approximately 2.47105 acres.

History

The hectare was introduced in the metric system in 1795 during the French Revolution as a practical unit for land measurement. The acre has medieval origins, historically defined as the area of land that could be plowed in one day by a yoke of oxen, and was standardized in the British Weights and Measures Act of 1824.
