Square Inches to Square Centimeters Converter

Definition

Square inch (in²) is an imperial and US customary unit of area, defined as the area of a square with sides of 1 inch. Square centimeter (cm²) is a metric unit of area, defined as the area of a square with sides of 1 centimeter. One square inch equals approximately 6.4516 square centimeters.

History

The square inch has been used in England since ancient times and was formalized in the imperial system. The square centimeter was established as part of the metric system during the French Revolution in the late 18th century. While the metric system is now used globally, the square inch remains common in the United States and some other countries for certain applications.
