Square Kilometers to Square Miles Converter

Definition

Square kilometer (km²) is a metric unit of area equal to 1,000,000 square meters or the area of a square with sides of 1 kilometer. Square mile (mi²) is an imperial and US customary unit equal to the area of a square with sides of 1 mile. One square kilometer equals approximately 0.3861 square miles.

History

The square kilometer was established as part of the metric system during the French Revolution in the late 18th century. The square mile has its origins in ancient Roman measurements and was adopted by the British Imperial System in the 19th century, remaining in use in the United States and a few other countries despite the widespread adoption of the metric system.
