Square Meters to Square Feet Converter

Definition

Square meter (m²) is the SI unit of area, defined as the area of a square with sides of 1 meter. Square foot (ft²) is an imperial and US customary unit of area, defined as the area of a square with sides of 1 foot. One square meter equals approximately 10.7639 square feet.

History

The square meter was established as part of the metric system during the French Revolution in the late 18th century. The square foot dates back to ancient civilizations, including the Romans, and was standardized in England in the 13th century, later adopted by the British Imperial System and the US customary system.
