Bits to Nibbles Converter

Definition

A bit (b) is the smallest unit of digital information, representing a binary value of 0 or 1. A nibble is a unit of digital storage equal to 4 bits, capable of representing one hexadecimal digit (0-15), occasionally used in early computing and low-level programming.
