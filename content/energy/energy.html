Purpose

The Energy Conversions category is designed for those working with energy—whether in scientific research, electrical engineering, thermodynamics, or mechanical systems—offering tools to convert between metric, imperial, and specialized units seamlessly.

History

Energy units evolved with scientific and industrial needs: joules honor James Prescott Joule’s 19th-century work, calories emerged from heat studies, kilowatt-hours from electrical utilities, BTU from British engineering, electronvolts from 20th-century physics, ergs from the CGS system, and foot-pounds from imperial mechanics. This category connects these origins.
