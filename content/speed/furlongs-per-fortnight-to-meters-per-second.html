Furlongs per Fortnight to Meters per Second Converter

Definition

Furlongs per fortnight (fpf) is an obscure unit of speed, where 1 furlong is 201.168 meters and 1 fortnight is 14 days (1,209,600 seconds), equaling about 0.0001663095 meters per second per fpf, often used humorously. Meters per second (m/s) is the SI unit of speed.

History

Furlongs per fortnight isn’t a historical unit but a playful combination—furlongs from medieval England (1/8 mile) and fortnights from Old English (14 nights)—popularized in the 20th century as a quirky example in physics education. Meters per second was established with the metric system in the 1790s, adopted by SI in 1960.
