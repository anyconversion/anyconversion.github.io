Kilometers per Hour to Miles per Hour Converter

Definition

Kilometers per hour (km/h) is a metric unit of speed, equal to 1,000 meters per 3,600 seconds, or about 0.27777778 meters per second, used globally for vehicular speed. Miles per hour (mph) is a US customary unit, equal to 1 mile (1,609.344 meters) per hour, or approximately 0.44704 meters per second.

History

Kilometers per hour emerged with the metric system in the 1790s, standardized for transportation in the 19th century as railways and cars spread. Miles per hour dates to the 17th century in Britain, tied to the statute mile, and became prominent in the US with the automobile age in the early 20th century.
