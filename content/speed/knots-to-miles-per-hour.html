Knots to Miles per Hour Converter

Definition

A knot (kn) is a unit of speed equal to one nautical mile per hour, approximately 1.852 kilometers per hour or 0.514444444 meters per second, used in aviation and maritime contexts. Miles per hour (mph) is 1 statute mile (1,609.344 meters) per hour, or about 0.44704 meters per second.

History

The knot originated in the 17th century from the log-line method, where knots on a rope measured ship speed, standardized with the nautical mile in the 1920s. Miles per hour developed in Britain in the 17th century, tied to the statute mile, and became widespread in the US with road travel in the 20th century.
