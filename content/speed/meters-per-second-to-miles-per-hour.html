Meters per Second to Miles per Hour Converter

Definition

Meters per second (m/s) is the SI unit of speed, defined as the distance of 1 meter traveled in 1 second, widely used in science and engineering. Miles per hour (mph) is a US customary unit, equal to 1 mile (1,609.344 meters) per hour, or approximately 0.44704 meters per second, common in transportation.

History

Meters per second was introduced with the metric system in the 1790s, formalized by the SI system in 1960 for universal scientific application. Miles per hour originated in Britain in the 17th century, based on the statute mile, and became prevalent in the US with the rise of automobiles in the 20th century.
