Hours to Days Converter

Definition

An hour (hr) is a unit of time equal to 60 minutes or 3,600 seconds. A day (d) is a unit of time equal to 24 hours, 1,440 minutes, or 86,400 seconds, representing one complete rotation of the Earth around its axis.

History

The concept of a 24-hour day originated in ancient Egypt around 3000 BCE, where they divided the day and night into 12 hours each. These hours initially varied in length according to the season. The Greeks later adopted this system, and the Romans spread it throughout their empire. The fixed-length hour we use today became standardized with the invention of mechanical clocks in medieval Europe, allowing for consistent time measurement regardless of season.
