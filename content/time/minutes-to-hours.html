Minutes to Hours Converter

Definition

A minute (min) is a unit of time equal to 60 seconds. An hour (hr) is a unit of time equal to 60 minutes or 3,600 seconds, representing 1/24 of a mean solar day.

History

The division of hours into 60 minutes and minutes into 60 seconds comes from the ancient Babylonian sexagesimal (base-60) system developed around 2000 BCE. This system was later adopted by the ancient Greeks and then passed down through history. The 24-hour day was established by the ancient Egyptians, who divided the day and night into 12 hours each, though these hours initially varied in length with the seasons.
