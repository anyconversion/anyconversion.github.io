Purpose

The Time Conversions category is designed for those working with time—whether for scheduling, scientific precision, astronomical observations, or historical analysis—offering tools to convert between everyday, microscopic, and specialized units effortlessly.

History

Time units have ancient and scientific roots: seconds and minutes from Babylonian sexagesimal systems, hours and days from early calendars, weeks from religious traditions, years from solar cycles, microseconds and nanoseconds from 20th-century tech, sidereal days from astronomy, fortnights from Old English, and lustrums from Roman five-year periods. This category spans these timelines.
