Liters to Gallons Converter

Definition

A liter is a metric unit of volume equal to 1 cubic decimeter or 1,000 cubic centimeters. A US gallon is a unit of volume equal to 3.78541 liters or 231 cubic inches. There is also an Imperial gallon used in the UK and some Commonwealth countries, which equals 4.54609 liters.

History

The liter was introduced in France in 1795 as part of the metric system. The US gallon evolved from the English wine gallon, which was standardized by Queen Anne in 1707. The Imperial gallon was established in 1824 in the British Weights and Measures Act, defined as the volume of 10 pounds of water at a specific temperature.
