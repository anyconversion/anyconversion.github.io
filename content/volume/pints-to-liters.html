Pints to Liters Converter

Definition

A US pint is a unit of volume equal to 16 US fluid ounces or approximately 0.473176 liters. A liter is a metric unit of volume equal to 1 cubic decimeter or 1,000 cubic centimeters. The Imperial pint (used in the UK) is larger at 0.568261 liters.

History

The pint has medieval origins, but the US pint was standardized in the early 19th century based on the wine gallon. The liter was introduced in France in 1795 as part of the metric system.
