Quarts to Gallons Converter

Definition

A US quart is a unit of volume equal to 32 US fluid ounces, 2 pints, or 0.946353 liters. A US gallon equals 4 quarts, 8 pints, or 3.78541 liters. In the Imperial system (UK), a quart is slightly larger at 1.13652 liters, and a gallon is 4.54609 liters.

History

The quart derives from the Latin "quartus" meaning fourth, as it represents a quarter of a gallon. Both US and Imperial versions evolved from English measurements, but diverged when the UK established the Imperial system in 1824. The US retained the older Queen Anne wine gallon (established 1707) standard, while the UK adopted the new Imperial gallon, creating the difference that exists today.
