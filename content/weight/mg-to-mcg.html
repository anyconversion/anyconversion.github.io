Milligrams to Micrograms Converter

Definition

A milligram is a unit of mass in the metric system equal to one-thousandth of a gram (10^-3 grams). A microgram is a unit of mass in the metric system equal to one-millionth of a gram (10^-6 grams). One milligram equals exactly 1,000 micrograms.

History

Both the milligram and microgram are part of the metric system that originated during the French Revolution in the late 18th century. The metric system was developed to establish a universal decimal-based system of measurement. The prefixes "milli-" (meaning one-thousandth) and "micro-" (meaning one-millionth) were standardized as part of the metric system's development. In 1960, the International System of Units (SI) formally incorporated these units with their modern definitions, establishing them as standard units for scientific, medical, and pharmaceutical measurements worldwide.
