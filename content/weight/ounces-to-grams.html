Ounces to Grams Converter

Definition

An ounce is a unit of weight in the imperial and US customary systems, equal to 1/16 of a pound or approximately 28.35 grams. A gram is a unit of mass in the metric system, equal to one-thousandth of a kilogram. One ounce equals approximately 28.3495 grams.

History

The ounce derives from the Roman "uncia," which was 1/12 of a Roman pound. In medieval England, various standards were used until the avoirdupois system became dominant, defining the ounce as 1/16 of a pound. The gram was introduced as part of the metric system during the French Revolution in the 1790s. It was originally defined as the mass of one cubic centimeter of water at the temperature of melting ice. The current international definitions were standardized in 1959, with the avoirdupois ounce defined as exactly 28.349523125 grams.
