Purpose

The Weight Conversions category is designed for those working with mass—whether in shipping, science, jewelry, or daily life—offering tools to convert between metric, imperial, and specialized units seamlessly.

History

Weight units evolved from trade and science: kilograms from the metric system, pounds and ounces from Roman and English traditions, grams and milligrams from precision needs, tons from maritime commerce, stone from British custom, carats from gem trading, slugs from physics, troy ounces from precious metals, and grains from ancient measures. This category unites these standards.
