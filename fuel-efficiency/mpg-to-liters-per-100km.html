MPG (US) to Liters per 100 km Converter

Definition

Miles per gallon (MPG, US) is a unit of fuel efficiency measuring the distance a vehicle can travel per US gallon of fuel, where 1 US gallon equals 3.785411784 liters. Liters per 100 kilometers (L/100 km) measures fuel consumption as the volume of fuel used to travel 100 kilometers, commonly used in metric countries.

History

MPG emerged in the US in the early 20th century with the rise of automobiles, standardized with the US gallon (smaller than the imperial gallon) for automotive testing.
