Purpose

The Length Conversions category is designed for those working with distance—whether in construction, navigation, science, or historical research—offering tools to convert between metric, imperial, and specialized units effortlessly.

History

Length units have ancient and practical origins: meters from the metric system, feet and inches from Anglo-Saxon measures, kilometers and miles from travel, centimeters and millimeters from precision, yards and rods from land surveying, nautical miles from maritime use, cubits from antiquity, furlongs and chains from agriculture, and light-years and parsecs from astronomy. This category spans these traditions.
