Purpose

The Speed Conversions category is designed for those working with velocity—whether in automotive, aviation, maritime, or scientific contexts—offering tools to convert between metric, imperial, and specialized units with ease.

History

Speed units evolved with human movement: kilometers and miles per hour from 19th-century travel, meters per second from metric science, knots from maritime navigation, Mach from 20th-century aviation, and furlongs per fortnight as a whimsical educational tool. This category spans these diverse roots.
