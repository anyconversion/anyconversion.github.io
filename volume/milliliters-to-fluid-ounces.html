Milliliters to Fluid Ounces Converter

Definition

A milliliter is one thousandth of a liter, equal to 1 cubic centimeter. A US fluid ounce is approximately 29.5735 milliliters. The Imperial fluid ounce (used in the UK) is slightly larger at 28.4131 milliliters.

History

The milliliter was established as part of the metric system during the French Revolution in the late 18th century. The fluid ounce has its origins in ancient Rome, but the modern US fluid ounce was defined in the early 19th century based on the wine gallon. The Imperial fluid ounce was standardized in 1824 with the British Weights and Measures Act.
