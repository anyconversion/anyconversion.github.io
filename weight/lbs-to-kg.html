Pounds to Kilograms Converter

Definition

A pound is a unit of weight in the imperial and US customary systems, equal to 16 ounces or exactly 0.45359237 kilograms. A kilogram is the base unit of mass in the International System of Units (SI), defined by physical constants since 2019. One pound equals exactly 0.45359237 kilograms.

History

The pound has ancient origins, deriving from the Roman "libra" (hence the abbreviation "lb"). The current international pound was standardized in 1959 by agreement between the United States and countries of the Commonwealth. The kilogram was introduced in France during the French Revolution in the 1790s as part of the metric system. Originally, it was defined as the mass of one liter of water.
